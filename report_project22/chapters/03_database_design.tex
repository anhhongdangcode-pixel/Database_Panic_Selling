\section{Relational Schema}

The database \texttt{fomo\_project} consists of the following six tables. Primary keys are underlined; foreign keys are indicated with (FK).

\subsection{Investors}

Stores investor profiles and psychological parameters used to drive simulation behavior.

\begin{table}[H]
    \centering
    \caption{Investors Table Schema}
    \begin{tabular}{llll}
        \toprule
        \textbf{Column} & \textbf{Type} & \textbf{Constraint} & \textbf{Description} \\
        \midrule
        Investor\_ID & VARCHAR(20) & PRIMARY KEY & Unique investor identifier \\
        Name & VARCHAR(100) & NOT NULL & Full name \\
        Investor\_Type & VARCHAR(20) & & FOMO / RATIONAL / NOISE \\
        Chasing\_Bias & FLOAT & & Tendency to buy on momentum \\
        Loss\_Aversion & FLOAT & & Sensitivity to losses \\
        Risk\_Appetite & FLOAT & & Willingness to take risk \\
        Impatience & FLOAT & & Tendency for impulsive decisions \\
        Initial\_Balance & DECIMAL(15,2) & & Starting capital (VND) \\
        \bottomrule
    \end{tabular}
\end{table}

\subsection{Market\_Data}

Historical OHLCV data with technical indicators for nine Vietnamese tickers.

\begin{table}[H]
    \centering
    \caption{Market\_Data Table Schema}
    \begin{tabular}{llll}
        \toprule
        \textbf{Column} & \textbf{Type} & \textbf{Constraint} & \textbf{Description} \\
        \midrule
        Date & DATE & PK (composite) & Trading date \\
        Ticker & VARCHAR(10) & PK (composite) & Stock ticker symbol \\
        Open / High / Low / Close & DECIMAL(15,2) & & OHLC prices \\
        Volume & BIGINT & & Daily volume \\
        MA\_20 & FLOAT & & 20-day moving average \\
        RSI\_14 & FLOAT & & 14-day RSI \\
        MA\_Volume\_30 & FLOAT & & 30-day average volume \\
        Percent\_Change & FLOAT & & Daily return (\%) \\
        \bottomrule
    \end{tabular}
\end{table}

\subsection{Transactions}

All buy and sell orders executed by investors during simulation.

\begin{table}[H]
    \centering
    \caption{Transactions Table Schema}
    \begin{tabular}{llll}
        \toprule
        \textbf{Column} & \textbf{Type} & \textbf{Constraint} & \textbf{Description} \\
        \midrule
        Trans\_ID & VARCHAR(20) & PRIMARY KEY & Unique transaction ID \\
        Investor\_ID & VARCHAR(20) & FK → Investors & Executing investor \\
        Date & DATE & & Transaction date \\
        Ticker & VARCHAR(10) & & Stock traded \\
        Action & VARCHAR(10) & & BUY or SELL \\
        Price & DECIMAL(15,2) & & Execution price \\
        Quantity & INT & & Number of shares \\
        Reason & VARCHAR(50) & & Regime\_Action label \\
        \bottomrule
    \end{tabular}
\end{table}

\subsection{Portfolio\_History}

Daily asset snapshots capturing total value, cash, and stock holdings per investor.

\subsection{BehaviorSignals}

Computed behavioral features and Panic Score per investor per observation period.

\begin{table}[H]
    \centering
    \caption{BehaviorSignals Table Schema}
    \begin{tabular}{llll}
        \toprule
        \textbf{Column} & \textbf{Type} & \textbf{Constraint} & \textbf{Description} \\
        \midrule
        SignalID & INT & AUTO\_INCREMENT PK & Unique signal record \\
        InvestorID & VARCHAR(20) & FK → Investors & Target investor \\
        ObservationDate & DATE & & Calculation date \\
        DrawdownLevel & FLOAT & & Max loss from peak \\
        SellSpike & FLOAT & & Sell intensity in PANIC regime \\
        LossSensitivity & FLOAT & & Response speed to losses \\
        PanicScore & FLOAT & & Aggregated score 0.0–1.0 \\
        \bottomrule
    \end{tabular}
\end{table}

\subsection{Warnings}

Advisory warnings automatically generated when Panic Score exceeds threshold.

\begin{table}[H]
    \centering
    \caption{Warnings Table Schema}
    \begin{tabular}{llll}
        \toprule
        \textbf{Column} & \textbf{Type} & \textbf{Constraint} & \textbf{Description} \\
        \midrule
        WarningID & INT & AUTO\_INCREMENT PK & Unique warning ID \\
        InvestorID & VARCHAR(20) & FK → Investors & Target investor \\
        WarningDate & DATE & & Date warning issued \\
        PanicLevel & VARCHAR(20) & & Low / Medium / High \\
        Confidence & FLOAT & & Model confidence 0.0–1.0 \\
        KeySignals & TEXT & & JSON string of signals \\
        \bottomrule
    \end{tabular}
\end{table}
